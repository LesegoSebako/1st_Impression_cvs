Maintenance & Updates – R499 /month

On-demand content edits, plugin updates, and troubleshooting.

Websites require regular attention to stay secure, functional, and aligned with your brand's evolving needs. Our Maintenance & Updates service ensures that your website remains up-to-date and performs smoothly without disruptions.

  • Plugin and software updates for better performance and security
  • Troubleshooting bugs, errors, and technical issues
  • Improved speed, responsiveness, and stability
  • Peace of mind knowing your site is always up-to-date
